Travel Trip & Camping

Our curated list of fun activities to do with us during your stay.

  • 26th August, 2023
  • Mama Susan

Thinking about mountain adventure travel? Have you put any thought into the best places to go when it comes to mountain adventure travel? Mount Elgon is one of the most popular places of all, when you visit this magical place you will have the best adventures right there at your doorstep.A great number of activities include:

Hiking

All the waterfalls on Mt. Elgon are found in different parts of the mountain. Elgon, you cannot see them together at once, but you can see them all in one day. As you walk on the hike, you should use walking sticks to help support you because there are some steep areas. There are different places to start the hike depending on which waterfall you want to see first. That's why I recommend hiking, which is a really cool experience. Plus, local guides will be with you and they know a lot about the history and geography of the area.

Abseiling & Rock climbing

Abseiling at Sipi Falls is very exciting and highly recommended. Climbing down from these amazing waterfalls is an incredible way to see them from a different angle and make a lasting memory. You can rappel down the highest waterfall, which is 100 meters tall, and enjoy the amazing views of the valley around it. The adventure can be adjusted to match different skill levels. It works for people who are just starting out and those who have a lot of experience. With experienced guides to keep you safe and make sure you have a good time, you can go abseiling without worrying.

Cultural Experiences

Right from Heritage Guesthouse our history and culture curator will give you a deep dive into the diverse culture of the sabiny people.A glimpse is male circumcision that is amazing and showcases the brave sons of the soil.Looking for a down to earth partner the sebei culture has groomed the best partners for stable family and friendship.Kapchorwa in the local language means home of friends. We also offer dining experiences with the natives to help diversify your cusine experience.

Birding/Bird Watching

Right at our guesthouse every morning in the luxurious rooms you can get to listen to the native bird species. Mountain Elgon National Park has over 200 bird species and they frequently visit the area. We help schedule visits to the Elgon national park for our guests.

Mountain Biking

Our location offers the best starting point for your biking escapades.

Guided mini marathons.

Looking to enjoy the same training routes used by the world champions who hail from the region. We offer the best routes based on your running experience. Come become a world champion with us.

Cave folktales

Looking for just a great folktale . We connect you with the best story tellers from the region. Join us for the 1000 story adventure as we also want to listen to your stories.We summarily document your heritage and story . Our vision is to ensure we curate as many stories from our guests.
